Recommender System


"Mr. Recommender" is systems represent user preferences for the purpose of suggesting items/deals purchase or examine. The main goal is to recommend a product or deal to a user based on their behavior. In our system, we collect some inputs from the users through an app. Using these inputs, a variety of techniques have applied for performing recommendations, including item-item collaborative filtering, user-user collaborative filtering, and matrix factorization.
